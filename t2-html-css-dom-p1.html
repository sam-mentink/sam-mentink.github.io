What is boxifying?

Boxifying is not about getting in the boxing ring with a website designer...

Boxifying is about looking at your potential layout and breaking it down in boxes - You would usually begin with the larger sections of your page first and break it down into smaller and smaller segments. Each section of your content can fit into a box... even if its a circle!

What is the Box Model?

The box model or CSS Box Model defines the HTML and refers to design and layout elements. The box model will define margins, borders, padding, the content and line weight.
