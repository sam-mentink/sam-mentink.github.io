    What is scope

    Scope is about variables and whether they are based inside or outside a function. If they are declared inside the function they are local, this means they cannot be accessed or effected by anything outside of that function, where as, if a variable is declared outside of a function it is therefor global and is able to be accessed or manipulated by other elements.
